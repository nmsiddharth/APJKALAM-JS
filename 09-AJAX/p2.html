PROMISES

What is Promise?

Promise is built-in constructor of javascript.

A Promise object represents an operation which has produced or will eventually produce a value.Promises povide a robust way to wrap the (possibly pending) result of asynchronous request.

States of Promises

1. Pending

The underlying operation has not yet completed or neither rejected.

2. Fulfilled

The operation has finished and promise is fulfilled with a response. (.then()) is used to handle fulfilled response.

3. Rejected

The error occured during the operation is rejected with a reason. (.catch()) is used to catch the error.

4. Settled

A promise is said to settled, (either resolved or rejected). Once the promise is settled , it becomes immutable.
